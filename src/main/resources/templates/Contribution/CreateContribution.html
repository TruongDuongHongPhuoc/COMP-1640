Term and Condition

As an educational institution committed to fostering academic integrity and excellence, it is imperative to establish a comprehensive long-term policy governing the submission of student work through our website. This policy aims to uphold the principles of fairness, transparency, and accountability while ensuring the security and integrity of all submitted files. Purpose: The purpose of this policy is to outline the guidelines and procedures for students when submitting their work through the institution's website. It also aims to provide clarity on the responsibilities of both students and the institution in maintaining academic standards and integrity. Submission Guidelines: a. File Formats: Students are required to submit their work in formats specified by the instructor or as outlined in the assignment guidelines. Commonly accepted formats include PDF, DOCX, or similar standard document formats. b. Naming Conventions: Students must adhere to specific naming conventions provided by the instructor or outlined in the assignment instructions. This helps in efficient organization and tracking of submissions. c. Submission Deadlines: Clear deadlines for file submissions will be communicated to students well in advance. Late submissions may be subject to penalties unless valid reasons are provided and approved by the instructor. Academic Integrity: a. Originality: Students are expected to submit original work that is their own and properly cite any sources used. Plagiarism in any form will not be tolerated and may result in disciplinary action. b. Collaboration: Collaboration policies should be clearly stated for each assignment. If collaboration is permitted, students must appropriately acknowledge their contributions and those of others. c. Integrity Violations: Any suspected violations of academic integrity will be thoroughly investigated, and appropriate actions will be taken as per the institution's academic integrity policy. File Security and Privacy: a. Data Encryption: All submitted files will be encrypted to ensure data security and privacy. b. Access Control: Access to submitted files will be restricted to authorized personnel only, such as instructors and designated administrators. c. Data Retention: Submitted files will be retained for a specified period as per the institution's data retention policy. After this period, files may be securely archived or deleted in compliance with relevant regulations. Technical Support: a. Submission Portal: The institution will provide a user-friendly submission portal where students can securely upload their files. b. Technical Assistance: Technical support will be available to assist students in case of any difficulties or issues encountered during the submission process. Feedback and Grades: a. Timely Feedback: Instructors are responsible for providing timely feedback on submitted work, adhering to the established grading timeline. b. Grade Confidentiality: Grades and feedback will be communicated to students in a confidential manner, respecting their privacy and academic standing. Policy Review and Updates: a. Regular Review: This policy will be periodically reviewed and updated as necessary to adapt to changes in technology, academic standards, or institutional requirements. b. Stakeholder Input: Input from students, faculty, and other stakeholders will be sought during policy review processes to ensure inclusivity and relevance. Compliance and Enforcement: a. Compliance: All students are expected to comply with this policy as a condition of their enrollment. b. Enforcement: Violations of this policy will be addressed through established disciplinary procedures, which may include academic sanctions or other appropriate measures.
